How it all began

A Tale of Two Hearts

2018
Paris • The Spark
Chapter One
Coffee & Clumsiness

It was a rather unremarkable rainy afternoon in a Parisian café. Unremarkable, until James managed to gracefully spill a scorching latte directly onto Emma’s favorite paperback. The frantic apologies that followed spiraled into a conversation so effortless, neither noticed when the rain finally stopped.

Chapter Two
Oceans Apart, Hearts Together

Five years of long-distance brought thousands of air miles, endless midnight FaceTime calls, and two rescued golden retrievers. They discovered that true connection doesn’t rely on physical proximity; it thrives in a deep, shared trust and unwavering commitment.

Chapter Three
The "Of Course" Moment

Last winter, precisely at the same spot the fateful coffee was spilled, James reached into his coat pocket. He hadn’t even opened the ring box completely before she was already screaming "Of Course!", tears freezing beautifully in the crisp December air.
